---
title: Flow of Time
description: The law of temporal consistency across different worlds.
order: 104
---

The rate at which time flows is exactly the same across different worlds. Whether in M3, M2, WF, or DR, the passage of time is consistent for \wkn{} and any OC. This means that an OC's age and experiences remain synchronized in every world, and no time difference arises because of the world they are in.

This is also called the **Law of Temporal Consistency** under \wkn{}'s worldview. This law ensures that the experiences and growth of the OCs across different worlds remain coherent, preventing temporal breaks or dislocations caused by switching worlds and avoiding the emergence of time paradoxes. The choices a character makes in one world, the experience they accumulate, and the consequences they bear will all be carried into other worlds in an equally real manner. Therefore, the multi-world structure does not weaken the continuity of character growth itself.
